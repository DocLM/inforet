Double Negative (or DNeg) is a British motion picture visual effects and computer animation company that was founded in 1998 in London.
The company has received three Academy Awards for work on the films Inception, Interstellar, and Ex Machina. In addition, Double Negative has received several Visual Effects Society awards for films such as Inception, Sherlock Holmes, and Interstellar, as well as BAFTA awards for Inception, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows â€“ Part 2 and Interstellar.
Double Negative is headquartered in Fitzrovia, London, with additional locations in Montreal, Vancouver, Mumbai, Chennai and Los Angeles.
Double negative became the primary visual effects team for Doctor Who in that show's eleventh series, succeeding Milk VFX.
